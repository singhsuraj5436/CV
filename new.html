#365daysofcode

I'm a student.

Punctuality: An ideal student is always punctual. He does everything on time.

Ambitious: He has a definite goal in life and works hard to achieve it.

Creativity: An ideal student is never a book-worm. He does other activities that make him creative.

Rationality and Logic: An Ideal Student does not believe in superstitions. He is always scientific, rational and logical...

Devotedness: He always does everything with devotion and hard work.
